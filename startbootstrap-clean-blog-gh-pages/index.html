Must-have Apple Products

According to Jared Phelan

iPhone 15 Pro

Reasons to buy:

  • Titanium frame makes for a much lighter phone
  • The latest installment of iPhone should receive iOS updates for years to come without issue
  • Optical zoom at 1x, 2x, and 3x on the Pro, and 1x, 2x, and 5x on the Pro Max

You can purchase the iPhone 15 Pro here

AirPods Pro 2nd Generation (USB-C charging)

Reasons to buy:

  • New H2 chip allows for both Dolby Atmos and Lossless Audio listening experiences
  • Seamless switching between Apple products such as iPhone, Mac, and iPad
  • 3 noise modes: Transparency Mode, Noise Cancellation, and the all-new Adaptive Audio

You can purchase AirPods Pro 2nd Generation here

Apple Watch Series 9

Reasons to buy:

  • Track calories, workouts, sleep, and much more without doing a thing!
  • Double tap feature for when your hands are full but you still need to take that call
  • Better quality of life, safety features, and oh yeah, it tells the time

You can purchase Apple Watch Series 9 here

M1 MacBook Pro (14-inch)

Reasons to buy:

  • Incredibly powerful and fast, despite 3 years of life
  • Magsafe charging makes for easy and fast charging, with battery life of up to 18 hours
  • All of the apps on your iPhone you love are on the MacBook as well, including iMessage and FaceTime

You can purchase the M1 MacBook Pro here
